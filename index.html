About Mazaca

Powered Land for Turkey's Digital Future

Global data centre operators are moving into Turkey, driven by
  • the country's data protection laws which mandate domestic data processing for large platforms,
  • the strong government-backed “Digital Türkiye” long-term agenda, and
  • its unique geographic position as a connectivity bridge between Europe, the Middle East and Asia.

The constraint is no longer demand — it is access to the right land, power and permits.

Mazaca Infrastructure develops industrial sites into Ready-to-Build data centre opportunities for operators, investors and digital infrastructure platforms.

We combine local origination, grid access, permitting expertise and institutional structuring to unlock the powered land required for Turkey’s next wave of digital infrastructure growth.

What We Do

A single value chain, end to end

Mazaca manages the full lifecycle from land to institutional-grade asset, removing risk at every stage so capital can deploy with confidence.

Site Identification

We source strategically located industrial land across Turkey's key digital markets, prioritising sites with existing grid infrastructure and backbone connectivity.

Power Securing

We lead the full TEİAŞ grid connection process, securing large-scale energy allocations with dual medium-voltage connections on behalf of the site owner.

Fiber & Connectivity

We verify and secure dual-path fiber connectivity from national backbone providers, assess carrier-neutral exchange proximity, and commission telecom infrastructure design — a non-negotiable condition for any institutional operator.

Permitting & Design

Zoning alignment, EIA completion, municipality approvals, and Tier III/IV conceptual design — all delivered to permit-ready standard with dual fibre secured.

Investor Exit

We market to global hyperscale operators and institutional platforms, structuring the exit through outright sale, long-term lease, or structured co-development.

Site Assessment Methodology

Site assessments are conducted with utmost care and diligence across the following primary evaluation headings — each site scored against a weighted matrix before any development commitment is made.

Section I — Power, Water & Infrastructure
  • ›Power supply security, substation redundancy & voltage levels
  • ›Industrial water availability & reliability
  • ›Renewable energy & sustainability
  • ›Telecommunications, fiber & backbone proximity
Section II — Risk & Geographic Suitability
  • ›Hydrological risks & flood history
  • ›Industrial hazards & airport flight paths
  • ›EMI / RFI risks & power line proximity
  • ›Topography, soil structure & expansion potential
Section III — Legal, Financial & Incentives
  • ›Zoning, permissions & building regulations
  • ›Energy incentives & operational power costs
  • ›Land acquisition model & cost (CAPEX / OPEX)
Section IV — Logistics & Operational Support
  • ›Transportation infrastructure
  • ›On-site operational support & regional amenities
  • ›Skilled workforce availability

For each site, Mazaca provides a model site layout plan, data centre building shell design, KMZ maps for the fiber infrastructure, and power substation details — all at Ready-to-Build standard.
